EA SPORTS UFC 4

EA SPORTS UFC 4 The game features a career mode, which was designed to be an "onboarding experience" to teach the player the basics of four MMA disciplines which include boxing, kickboxing, wrestling, and jiujitsu. The goal of the career mode, which allows players to create their own custom fighter, is to become the G.O.A.T. UFC 4 serves up a multitude of modes to fight your way through, but the heart of any UFC game lies in its combat mechanics; and they're better than ever. While much of the standing game has carried over from UFC 3, strikes still feel satisfying and weighty, with an added emphasis on striking distance.
