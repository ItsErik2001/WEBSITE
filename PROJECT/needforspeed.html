NEED FOR SPEED

Need For Speed is a racing game set in an open world environment called Palm City, a fictionalised version of Miami, Florida and its surrounding area. During the night, players can take part in illicit street racing, which rewards REP. Need For Speed Heat provides a fantastic escape for those who'd love to live out their fantasies of being in control of cars they'll probably never be able to afford. For a sale price of $30, this game is definitely worth it.
