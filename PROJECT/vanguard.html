VANGUARD

CALL OF DUTY: VANGUARD campaign is set in World War II and features battles from all four fronts. That's Western, Eastern, the Pacific, and North Africa.Compared to Black Ops Cold War, Vanguard has a vastly improved multiplayer that feels like a solid foundation for Sledgehammer Games to build on throughout the seasons of post-launch content but with an underwhelming campaign and Zombies mode that seemingly needs a lot of work to win back the hardcore fans
