PGA TOUR 2K21

PGA TOUR 2K21 is a sports video game developed by HB Studios and published by 2K Sports for Stadia, Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. It's a continuation of The Golf Club franchise integrated with 2K Sports and the second game in the franchise to include a PGA Tour license.From a pure gameplay standpoint, PGA Tour 2K21 delivers a strong enough experience to satisfy fans, and the course designer is fantastic. However, the game falls more toward the average because of its bland career mode, modest list of licensed golfers, and out-dated graphics.
